Started From A Box

In "Started From A Box", Corneezius, AKA "Cornelius Range V", codes a poetic origin story to open his self-named debut album. "I Like Rocks Pt.1" tells a somewhat true story about coked up Rocks, a racist potato, and a police sting gone awry in Memphis, TN. In "She Don't Luv You",Corneezius offers sage love advice to fellow romantics. In "Banners", the first poem authored in this collection, he talks about some real heavy shit. In "I Like Rocks Pt.2", The Biggest Rock Since Mars ethers his foes and claims the world as his stage.
